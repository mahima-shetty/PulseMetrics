# Revenue Growth Strategies for Startups

## Finding Product-Market Fit
Before scaling revenue, ensure your product solves a real problem for a defined audience. Talk to at least 50 customers. If they wouldn't be disappointed if your product disappeared, you don't have fit yet.

## Pricing and Packaging
- Test multiple price points; many startups underprice by 2-3x.
- Consider usage-based or value-based pricing instead of flat fees.
- Offer annual plans with 15-20% discount to improve cash flow and reduce churn.

## Sales and Distribution
- Focus on one channel before expanding: direct sales, partnerships, or self-serve.
- For B2B: shorten sales cycles by offering pilots, trials, or money-back guarantees.
- For B2C: invest in referral programs and community building.

## Revenue Metrics to Track
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) and growth rate
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Revenue concentration: avoid depending on a single customer for >20% of revenue
