# Customer Retention and Churn Prevention

## Why Retention Matters
Acquiring a new customer costs 5-25x more than retaining an existing one. A 5% increase in retention can increase profits by 25-95%.

## Early Warning Signs of Churn
- Declining login frequency or feature usage
- Support tickets with unresolved complaints
- Payment failures or downgrade requests
- Delayed renewal discussions

## Retention Tactics
- **Onboarding**: First 30 days are critical. Proactive check-ins and success milestones.
- **Health scores**: Combine usage, NPS, and support sentiment into a single metric.
- **Win-back campaigns**: Email or call customers who haven't used the product in 30-60 days.
- **Exit surveys**: When a customer churns, ask why. Use insights to fix systemic issues.

## Measuring Retention
- Cohort analysis: Track retention by signup month.
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR): Expansion minus churn; aim for >100%.
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) vs. CAC: LTV should be at least 3x CAC.
